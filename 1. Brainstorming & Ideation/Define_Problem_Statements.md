# Define Problem Statements — Customer Problem Statement Template

*Create a problem statement to understand your customer's point of view: I am (customer) ... I'm trying to ... but (constraint) ... because (reason) ... which makes me feel ...*

| Problem Statement (PS) | I am (Customer) | I'm trying to | But | Because | Which makes me feel |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| PS-1 | A university student revising for exams | quickly understand a dense textbook topic | I don't have time to read the whole chapter | the explanation is buried in long, jargon-heavy paragraphs | overwhelmed and behind schedule |
| PS-2 | A self-learner studying outside class hours | test myself on what I just read | I have no one to make me a quiz on demand | manually writing questions takes too long | unsure whether I actually understood the material |
| PS-3 | A student with unreliable or expensive internet | keep studying even when my connection drops | most AI study tools stop working offline | they depend entirely on cloud APIs | frustrated and unable to keep a consistent study routine |
| PS-4 | An educator preparing a lesson | generate evaluation questions from a reading passage | I spend hours manually drafting MCQs | there's no quick automated tool integrated into my workflow | pressed for time and prone to skipping proper assessment |
| PS-5 | A student on a tight budget | use AI help for every small task (definitions, summaries) | heavy reliance on paid cloud LLM APIs adds up fast | every request — even a simple one — costs tokens | anxious about ongoing subscription/API costs |

**Summary:** These statements point to one root need — a single tool that gives students and educators fast, low-cost, resilient AI assistance across the full study cycle (explain → summarize → quiz → plan), without forcing every micro-task through an expensive cloud API.
